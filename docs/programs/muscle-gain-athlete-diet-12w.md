# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Muscle Gain Athlete Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Build Muscle + Improve Performance + Stay Lean  
Nutrition Style: Athlete Lean Gain  
Daily Calories: ~2,800-3,000 kcal  
Hydration: 3.5-4L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week athlete-focused nutrition system for lean muscle gain and better training performance.
- Combines high protein, high-performance carbs, and controlled fats for clean growth.
- Built to support recovery quality while minimizing unnecessary fat gain.
- Uses structured meal rotation to keep consistency high through the full cycle.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Athlete Power Oats  
  70g oats, 1 scoop whey, 250ml milk, 1 banana, 10 almonds, cinnamon  
  Approx macros: 35P / 85C / 18F
- **Lunch:** Chicken Rice Performance Bowl  
  180g chicken, 120g cooked rice, mixed vegetables, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika  
  Approx macros: 50P / 80C / 20F
- **Dinner:** Salmon and Sweet Potato (Recovery Meal)  
  150g salmon, 200g sweet potato, 1 tsp olive oil, salt, garlic  
  Approx macros: 40P / 60C / 25F
- **Snack:** Yogurt, Honey, and Nuts  
  200g yogurt, 1 tsp honey, 15g nuts  
  Approx macros: 20P / 25C / 15F

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Eggs and Oats Combo  
  3 eggs, 50g oats, 200ml milk  
  Approx macros: 30P / 60C / 20F
- **Lunch:** Beef Rice Athlete Bowl  
  180g beef, 120g rice, vegetables, 1 tsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 45P / 80C / 20F
- **Dinner:** Chicken Pasta (Clean Bulk)  
  180g chicken, 100g pasta, 1 tsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 50P / 80C / 15F

### Days 3-7
- **Day 3:** Athlete oats / Chicken + rice / Salmon + sweet potato
- **Day 4:** Eggs + oats / Beef + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 5:** Athlete oats / Chicken + rice / Salmon + sweet potato
- **Day 6:** Eggs + oats / Beef + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 7:** Athlete oats / Chicken + rice / Salmon + sweet potato

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~170g protein / ~330g carbs / ~75g fat
- **Water:** 3.5-4L daily
- **Primary objective:** build a clean athlete surplus with strong performance support

## Week 1 Quick Recipe Standards
- **Athlete Oats:** cook oats and milk on medium heat for about 5 min; remove from heat, then add whey, cinnamon, banana, and almonds.
- **Chicken Performance Bowl:** season and cook chicken 5-6 min each side on medium-high heat; cook rice 10-12 min; steam vegetables about 5 min; finish with olive oil.
- **Salmon Recovery Meal:** bake sweet potato at 200C for 25-30 min; bake salmon at 180C for 12-15 min; finish with olive oil and garlic.
- **Chicken Pasta:** boil pasta 8-10 min, cook chicken separately, combine and finish with olive oil.

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Athlete Base)
- Establish consistent meal timing and hydration.
- Build stable clean surplus for performance and growth.
- Expect early strength improvements.

### Weeks 3-6 (Performance Growth)
- Maintain high protein and clean carb structure.
- Improve gym output and recovery quality.
- Keep intake consistent across training and rest days.

### Weeks 7-10 (Lean Gain Refinement)
- Continue growth while controlling fat gain pace.
- Preserve food quality and avoid dirty-bulk patterns.
- Adjust portions only based on trend data.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Athlete Lean Gain)
- Finalize with strict consistency and high-quality recovery nutrition.
- Aim for stronger lifts, fuller muscles, and lean athletic look.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Core items: oats, whey, milk, eggs, chicken (~1.8kg), beef (~1kg), salmon (3-4 pieces), rice, pasta, sweet potatoes, yogurt, almonds/nuts, honey, vegetables, olive oil.
- Batch-prep proteins and carb sources for reliable compliance.
- Keep a recovery meal ready after harder sessions.

## Alternatives
- Salmon -> tuna or chicken
- Beef -> chicken thighs
- Rice -> potatoes or quinoa
- Milk -> water or almond milk
- Oats -> whole grain bread
- Yogurt -> milk or kefir

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein
- Creatine
- Omega-3
- Electrolytes

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** improved strength and training quality
- **Weeks 3-6:** increased muscle fullness
- **Weeks 8-12:** visible lean muscle gain

## Safety Protocol
- Do not force excess calories if digestion quality drops.
- Maintain hydration, electrolytes, and sleep consistency.
- Keep growth clean and adjust surplus gradually as needed.
