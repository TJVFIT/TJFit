# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Student Bulk Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Gain Muscle Cheaply  
Nutrition Style: Budget Bulk  
Daily Calories: ~2,700-3,000 kcal  
Hydration: 3-3.5L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week budget-focused bulk nutrition system for affordable muscle gain.
- Uses simple, accessible foods to maintain a calorie surplus without high costs.
- Keeps protein high enough for growth while relying on cheap carb staples.
- Built for students who need consistency, size, and value.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Budget Oat Mass Bowl  
  60g oats, 250ml milk, 1 banana, 1 tbsp peanut butter  
  Approx macros: 20P / 85C / 15F
- **Lunch:** Cheap Chicken Rice  
  150g chicken, 120g rice, salt, paprika  
  Approx macros: 40P / 80C / 8F
- **Dinner:** Egg and Potato Bulk Plate  
  4 eggs, 250g potatoes, salt  
  Approx macros: 30P / 70C / 20F
- **Snack:** Milk and Bread  
  300ml milk, 2 slices bread  
  Approx macros: 15P / 50C / 10F

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Eggs and Bread  
  3 eggs, 2 slices bread  
  Approx macros: 25P / 50C / 18F
- **Lunch:** Tuna Rice Bulk  
  1-2 cans tuna, 120g rice  
  Approx macros: 40P / 80C / 6F
- **Dinner:** Chicken and Potato  
  150g chicken, 250g potato  
  Approx macros: 40P / 70C / 8F

### Days 3-7
- **Day 3:** Oats bowl / Chicken + rice / Eggs + potatoes
- **Day 4:** Eggs + bread / Tuna + rice / Chicken + potatoes
- **Day 5:** Oats bowl / Chicken + rice / Eggs + potatoes
- **Day 6:** Eggs + bread / Tuna + rice / Chicken + potatoes
- **Day 7:** Oats bowl / Chicken + rice / Eggs + potatoes

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~140-160g protein / ~350g carbs / ~70g fat
- **Water:** 3-3.5L daily
- **Primary objective:** maintain affordable calorie surplus for muscle growth

## Week 1 Quick Recipe Standards
- **Budget Oat Bowl:** cook oats with milk on medium heat for about 5 min, then add banana and peanut butter.
- **Cheap Chicken Rice:** season chicken with salt and paprika, cook 5-6 min each side, boil rice 10-12 min, combine.
- **Egg and Potato Plate:** boil potatoes for 15-20 min, cook eggs on medium heat around 3 min, season.
- **Simple Snack:** use milk and bread to close calorie gaps on busy days.

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Budget Bulk Base)
- Build consistency with low-cost surplus foods.
- Establish stable meal prep and daily hydration.
- Expect early weight increase.

### Weeks 3-6 (Growth Consistency)
- Keep meal repetition for easier adherence.
- Maintain cheap protein and carb staples.
- Drive strength increases through reliable intake.

### Weeks 7-10 (Reinforcement)
- Continue surplus while improving routine quality.
- Prevent skipped meals and under-eating.
- Keep gains productive and controlled.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Student Bulk)
- Finish with full consistency and stable intake.
- Target visible size and stronger gym performance.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Core items: oats, milk, eggs (30+), chicken (~1.5kg), tuna cans, rice (1-2kg), potatoes (2-3kg), bread, peanut butter, salt, basic spices.
- Batch-cook rice, potatoes, and chicken to reduce missed meals.
- Keep fallback cheap options ready for schedule-heavy days.

## Alternatives
- Chicken -> eggs or tuna
- Tuna -> eggs
- Rice -> pasta or bread
- Milk -> water (less calories)
- Peanut butter -> skip or use oil
- Potatoes -> rice

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein (if budget allows)
- Creatine

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** bodyweight increase
- **Weeks 3-6:** strength increase
- **Weeks 8-12:** visible muscle gain

## Safety Protocol
- Avoid relying only on low-quality calories.
- Increase food volume gradually if digestion is stressed.
- Keep hydration high during surplus phases.
