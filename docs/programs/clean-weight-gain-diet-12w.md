# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Clean Weight Gain Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Gain Muscle with Minimal Fat (Clean Bulk)  
Nutrition Style: Controlled Clean Surplus  
Daily Calories: ~2,800-3,000 kcal  
Hydration: 3.5-4L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week clean bulk nutrition system for lean, aesthetic muscle gain.
- Uses controlled surplus strategy with high-quality whole-food meals.
- Built to improve performance while minimizing unnecessary fat gain.
- Focuses on structure, consistency, and recovery support.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Clean Muscle Oats  
  70g oats, 1 scoop whey, 250ml milk, 1 banana, 1 tbsp peanut butter, cinnamon  
  Approx macros: 35P / 80C / 18F
- **Lunch:** Clean Chicken Rice Plate  
  180g chicken breast, 120g cooked rice, broccoli, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika  
  Approx macros: 50P / 80C / 20F
- **Dinner:** Salmon and Sweet Potato Clean Bulk  
  150g salmon, 200g sweet potato, 1 tsp olive oil, salt, garlic  
  Approx macros: 40P / 60C / 25F
- **Snack:** Yogurt, Nuts, and Honey  
  200g yogurt, 1 tsp honey, 15g nuts  
  Approx macros: 20P / 25C / 15F

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Eggs and Oats Combo  
  3 eggs, 50g oats, 200ml milk  
  Approx macros: 30P / 60C / 20F
- **Lunch:** Beef Rice Clean Bulk  
  180g beef, 120g rice, vegetables, 1 tsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 45P / 80C / 20F
- **Dinner:** Chicken Pasta Clean Meal  
  180g chicken, 100g pasta, 1 tsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 50P / 80C / 15F

### Days 3-7
- **Day 3:** Clean oats / Chicken + rice / Salmon + sweet potato
- **Day 4:** Eggs + oats / Beef + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 5:** Clean oats / Chicken + rice / Salmon + sweet potato
- **Day 6:** Eggs + oats / Beef + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 7:** Clean oats / Chicken + rice / Salmon + sweet potato

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~170g protein / ~320g carbs / ~75g fat
- **Water:** 3.5-4L daily
- **Primary objective:** establish clean surplus for lean muscle growth

## Week 1 Quick Recipe Standards
- **Clean Muscle Oats:** cook oats with milk 5 min on medium heat; remove from heat; add whey, cinnamon, banana, and peanut butter.
- **Chicken Rice Plate:** season and cook chicken 5-6 min each side on medium-high heat; cook rice 10-12 min; steam broccoli 4-5 min; finish with olive oil.
- **Salmon and Sweet Potato:** bake sweet potato at 200C for 25-30 min; bake salmon at 180C for 12-15 min; finish with garlic and olive oil.
- **Chicken Pasta Meal:** boil pasta 8-10 min; cook chicken separately; combine and finish with oil.

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Clean Gain Base)
- Build consistent meal timing and hydration.
- Establish stable surplus with clean foods.
- Expect slight bodyweight increase.

### Weeks 3-6 (Lean Growth Block)
- Maintain protein and performance carb structure.
- Improve strength and training output.
- Keep recovery quality high.

### Weeks 7-10 (Aesthetic Reinforcement)
- Continue muscle gain while controlling fat trend.
- Preserve food quality and avoid dirty-bulk drift.
- Use measured adjustments only when needed.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Clean Gain)
- Finish with strict consistency and clear physique progress.
- Target fuller muscles with lean visual quality.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Core items: oats, whey, milk, eggs, chicken (~2kg), beef (~1kg), salmon (3-4 pieces), rice, pasta, sweet potatoes, yogurt, nuts, honey, vegetables, olive oil.
- Batch-cook proteins and carbs for reliable adherence.
- Keep clean snack options ready for schedule-heavy days.

## Alternatives
- Salmon -> tuna or chicken
- Beef -> chicken thighs
- Rice -> potatoes or quinoa
- Milk -> almond milk or water
- Oats -> whole grain bread
- Yogurt -> kefir or milk

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** slight weight increase
- **Weeks 3-6:** stronger training output and muscle fullness
- **Weeks 8-12:** lean muscle growth with controlled fat gain

## Safety Protocol
- Avoid uncontrolled dirty-bulk calories.
- Increase intake gradually if digestion is stressed.
- Maintain hydration and sleep consistency.
