# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## 12-Week Hypertrophy System

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Gym Training  
Primary Goal: Maximum Muscle Growth (Size + Fullness)  
Training Style: High Reps + Volume + Pump  
Frequency: 5-6 Days / Week  
Equipment: Full Gym  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week hypertrophy program focused on muscle fullness and visual size.
- Uses a full split with high-rep, high-volume execution across all groups.
- Progresses through superset phases, drop sets, and peak pump loading.
- Built for advanced training density while preserving strict movement quality.

## Weekly Training Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Pump Foundation)
1. Day 1: Chest pump.
2. Day 2: Back pump.
3. Day 3: Leg pump.
4. Day 4: Shoulder pump.
5. Day 5: Arms pump.
6. Day 6: Optional light cardio.
7. Day 7: Rest.

### Weeks 3-4 (Volume Build)
1. Week 3 increases split days to 4 sets.
2. Week 4 keeps 4 sets with controlled rest windows.
3. Rep ranges stay hypertrophy-focused with strict form.

### Weeks 5-6 (Pump Phase)
1. Split sessions move to strategic superset structures.
2. Metabolic stress and time-under-tension are elevated.
3. Week 6 progresses to 5 sets.

### Weeks 7-8 (High Volume)
1. Rep and set demand increase across all muscle groups.
2. Week 8 adds finisher:
   - Push-ups max reps
   - Lateral Raises x 20
   - Repeat 2 rounds

### Weeks 9-10 (Extreme Pump)
1. Drop-set methods are introduced on key movements.
2. Split maintains high-volume hypertrophy emphasis.
3. Week 10 increases to 6 sets.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Pump)
1. Week 11 introduces double drop sets on final key sets.
2. Week 12 keeps 6-set peak structure.
3. Optional light pump day supports circulation and recovery.

## Coaching Notes (Premium Delivery Standard)
- Keep contractions controlled and intentional for maximum pump quality.
- Prioritize range of motion and tempo before adding extra load.
- Use drop sets as a precision tool, not a form-breakdown tool.
- Track per-muscle weekly volume to ensure balanced growth.

## Safety Protocol
- High volume increases fatigue; keep form strict.
- Stop immediately if technique quality drops.
- Recover aggressively between sessions.
