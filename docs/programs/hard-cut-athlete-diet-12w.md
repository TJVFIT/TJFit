# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Hard Cut Athlete Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Very Lean Athletic Physique (Low Body Fat)  
Nutrition Style: Aggressive Hard Cut  
Daily Calories: ~1,600-1,700 kcal  
Hydration: 3.5-4L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week aggressive cutting nutrition system designed for athletic definition.
- Keeps protein very high to protect muscle during a steep calorie deficit.
- Uses low-fat, controlled-carb meal structure for maximum fat-loss efficiency.
- Built for disciplined execution and peak physique outcomes.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Egg white protein scramble with spinach and whole-grain toast.
- **Lunch:** Lean chicken and rice athlete plate with broccoli.
- **Dinner:** White fish with zucchini and carrots.
- **Snack:** Lean Greek yogurt.

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Light protein oats.
- **Lunch:** Tuna lean bowl with rice and lettuce.
- **Dinner:** Chicken and sweet potato (cut version).

### Days 3-7
- Repeat hard-cut templates with egg whites, lean chicken, tuna, white fish, measured rice/sweet potato, and vegetables.
- Keep cooking simple, portions strict, and hydration high.

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~160g protein / ~120g carbs / ~40g fat
- **Water:** 3.5-4L daily
- **Primary objective:** fast fat-loss start while preserving lean tissue

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Aggressive Base)
- Establish strict meal compliance and hydration discipline.
- Build high-protein routine with low variability.
- Expect fast initial bodyweight reduction.

### Weeks 3-6 (Controlled Hard Cut)
- Sustain deficit with precise portions and clean food quality.
- Keep performance-supportive carbs measured and consistent.
- Preserve muscle with stable protein distribution.

### Weeks 7-10 (Athletic Definition)
- Tighten consistency under fatigue pressure.
- Keep meal timing aligned with training/recovery.
- Avoid unplanned substitutions and random intake.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Condition)
- Finalize with precision and repeatability.
- Maintain hydration and recovery support.
- Aim for sharp, defined, low body-fat finish.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Core foods: egg whites/eggs, chicken, white fish, tuna, rice, sweet potatoes, oats, whey, Greek yogurt, spinach, broccoli, zucchini, carrots, lettuce, olive oil, lemon.
- Batch-cook proteins and starches to reduce decision fatigue.
- Keep spice profile clean: salt, pepper, paprika, garlic, lemon.

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein
- Creatine
- Caffeine
- Electrolytes

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** fast weight drop
- **Weeks 3-6:** clear fat-loss progression
- **Weeks 8-12:** very lean, defined, athletic look

## Safety Protocol
- Aggressive deficits can increase fatigue quickly; monitor recovery.
- Keep protein and hydration consistent to protect performance.
- Reduce stress load if sleep, mood, or output drops sharply.
