# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Student Fat Loss Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Budget-Friendly Fat Loss  
Nutrition Style: Low-Cost High-Compliance  
Daily Calories: ~1,700-1,800 kcal  
Hydration: 3L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week fat-loss nutrition plan designed for students on a tight budget.
- Uses simple, cheap staple foods while keeping protein intake practical.
- Built around repeatable meals to reduce cost, stress, and inconsistency.
- Focused on discipline and sustainable body-composition improvement.

## Week 1 Premium Budget Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Cheap Protein Eggs and Bread  
  3 eggs, 1 slice bread, salt, pepper  
  Approx macros: 20P / 25C / 18F
- **Lunch:** Budget Chicken and Rice  
  120g chicken, 100g rice, salt, paprika  
  Approx macros: 35P / 50C / 6F
- **Dinner:** Tuna and Potato Bowl  
  1 can tuna, 200g potato, salt  
  Approx macros: 30P / 50C / 4F
- **Snack:** Yogurt  
  150g yogurt  
  Approx macros: 8P / 10C / 4F

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Oats and Milk  
  40g oats, 200ml milk  
  Approx macros: 15P / 40C / 8F
- **Lunch:** Eggs and Rice  
  3 eggs, 100g rice  
  Approx macros: 25P / 50C / 15F
- **Dinner:** Chicken and Bread  
  120g chicken, 1 slice bread  
  Approx macros: 35P / 25C / 6F

### Days 3-7
- **Day 3:** Eggs + bread / Chicken + rice / Tuna + potato
- **Day 4:** Oats + milk / Eggs + rice / Chicken + potato
- **Day 5:** Eggs + bread / Chicken + rice / Tuna + bread
- **Day 6:** Oats + milk / Eggs + rice / Chicken + potato
- **Day 7:** Eggs + bread / Chicken + rice / Tuna + potato

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~120-140g protein / ~160g carbs / ~45g fat
- **Water:** 3L daily
- **Primary objective:** start fat loss without increasing food budget

## Week 1 Quick Recipe Standards
- **Eggs + Bread:** medium pan heat, cook eggs 2-3 min, toast bread, season simply.
- **Chicken + Rice:** season chicken with salt and paprika, cook 5-6 min per side, boil rice 10-12 min.
- **Tuna + Potato:** boil potato 15-20 min, drain tuna, combine and season.
- **Oats + Milk:** simmer 4-5 min while stirring for better texture.

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Budget Foundation)
- Lock in a cheap and repeatable structure.
- Keep calories controlled and protein adequate.
- Focus on daily compliance rather than variety.

### Weeks 3-6 (Consistency Block)
- Buy staples in bulk and prep ahead.
- Keep meal timing stable and avoid random snacking.
- Sustain calorie deficit with practical food choices.

### Weeks 7-10 (Discipline Phase)
- Preserve adherence despite limited menu variety.
- Tighten portions when needed.
- Maintain routine around study/work demands.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Budget Cut)
- Finalize with strict consistency.
- Keep food simple, measurable, and affordable.
- Cement discipline habits for long-term fat-loss maintenance.

## Shopping and Cost Notes
- Primary staples: eggs (30+), chicken (~1.2kg), tuna cans, rice (1kg), potatoes (1-2kg), bread, oats, milk, yogurt, basic spices.
- Use alternatives based on weekly price changes.
- Meal prep in batches to reduce daily spending and missed meals.

## Alternatives
- Chicken -> eggs or tuna
- Tuna -> eggs
- Rice -> pasta or bread
- Milk -> water
- Yogurt -> skip or replace with eggs

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein (if budget allows)
- Multivitamin

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** bodyweight starts dropping
- **Weeks 3-6:** visible fat-loss trend
- **Weeks 8-12:** leaner shape with stronger food discipline

## Safety Protocol
- Do not crash calories if concentration and energy drop too hard.
- Keep hydration and protein intake consistent.
- Adjust portions gradually, not drastically.
