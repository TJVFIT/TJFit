# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Keto Shred Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Burn Fat, Preserve Muscle, Build Discipline  
Nutrition Style: Keto Shred  
Daily Calories: ~1,800-1,900 kcal  
Hydration: 3-3.5L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week ketogenic nutrition system designed for aggressive but sustainable fat loss.
- Focuses on low-carb consistency, protein preservation, and practical daily meal execution.
- Week 1 includes upgraded high-flavor recipes to improve adherence and long-term compliance.
- Built to keep food simple, repeatable, and effective for a leaner physique outcome.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Egg and avocado plate with olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika.
- **Lunch:** Juicy grilled chicken salad with garlic-paprika seasoning and olive oil finish.
- **Dinner:** Butter beef with garlic broccoli.
- **Snack:** Almonds (20g).

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Fluffy cheese omelette.
- **Lunch:** Creamy tuna salad with mayo and optional lemon.
- **Dinner:** Chicken and zucchini stir fry with garlic seasoning.

### Day 3
- **Breakfast:** Butter eggs.
- **Lunch:** Beef salad.
- **Dinner:** Chicken and broccoli.

## Week 1 Recipe Execution Standards
- Pan heat control is mandatory for texture and flavor.
- Rest proteins after cooking to retain juiciness.
- Use spice structure daily: salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, optional chili flakes, lemon.
- Prioritize taste quality to protect adherence.

## 12-Week Keto Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Adaptation)
- Transition into fat-based fuel utilization.
- Manage hydration and electrolytes carefully.
- Appetite often stabilizes as carb dependence drops.

### Weeks 3-6 (Fat-Loss Acceleration)
- Maintain strict meal consistency and portion control.
- Keep protein high to preserve muscle while cutting.
- Track body changes weekly and adjust only if adherence is strong.

### Weeks 7-10 (Lean Refinement)
- Tighten meal precision without extreme restriction.
- Keep recipe quality high to prevent diet fatigue.
- Continue hydration and recovery consistency.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Discipline)
- Final execution phase focused on clean compliance.
- Maintain stable intake and avoid random food additions.
- Finish with strong routine adherence and sustainable habits.

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein (post-workout)
- Omega-3
- Electrolytes (strongly recommended on keto)
- Multivitamin

## Expected Results Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** Fast initial drop (often water and early fat reduction)
- **Weeks 3-6:** Visible fat-loss momentum
- **Weeks 8-12:** Leaner and more defined look with consistency

## Safety Protocol
- If dizziness, unusual fatigue, or weakness appears, reduce training stress and review hydration/electrolytes.
- Never cut calories aggressively below sustainable levels.
- Prioritize consistency over extreme restriction.
