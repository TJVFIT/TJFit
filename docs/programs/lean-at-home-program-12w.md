# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Lean at Home Program (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Home Training  
Primary Goal: Fat Loss + Lean, Toned Physique  
Training Style: Cardio + Bodyweight Sculpting  
Frequency: 5-6 Days / Week  
Equipment: None  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week sculpting-focused program designed to reduce fat and improve muscle tone.
- Combines bodyweight shaping circuits with progressive cardio sessions.
- Uses round progression, rest reduction, and timed HIIT phases.
- Built for steady progression from beginner baseline to peak conditioning.

## Weekly Training Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Tone and Cardio Base)
1. Day 1 and Day 4: Full body tone circuit (squats, push-ups, sit-ups, plank).
2. Day 2 and Day 5: Cardio burn circuit (jumping jacks, high knees, mountain climbers).
3. Day 3: Walk 25-30 min.
4. Day 6: Walk 30 min.
5. Day 7: Rest.

### Weeks 3-4 (Volume Upgrade)
1. Week 3 increases round totals on both tone and cardio days.
2. Week 4 increases reps and hold durations.
3. Rest windows tighten for higher density output.
4. Day 6 introduces light jog + walk blend.

### Weeks 5-6 (Shaping Phase)
1. Pair 1: Squats -> Pulse Squats.
2. Pair 2: Push-ups -> Shoulder Taps.
3. Pair 3: Sit-ups -> Leg Raises.
4. Week 6 increases total rounds and cardio volume.

### Weeks 7-8 (Lean Focus)
1. Main shaping movements scale in reps and hold times.
2. Cardio rounds increase with higher interval duration.
3. Week 8 adds daily finisher:
   - Burpees x 10
   - Plank 40 sec
   - Repeat 3 rounds

### Weeks 9-10 (HIIT Sculpt)
1. Day 1 uses 40 sec work / 20 sec rest for sculpting circuit.
2. Day 2 uses 40/20 for cardio drills.
3. Week 10 advances to 7 rounds.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Sculpt)
1. Week 11 adds finisher:
   - Jump Squats x 12
   - Burpees x 12
   - Repeat 3 rounds
2. Week 12 model:
   - 45 sec work / 15 sec rest
   - 7 rounds
3. Support conditioning peaks with walk 45 min and jog 25 min.

## Coaching Notes (Premium Delivery Standard)
- Keep all reps controlled to maximize sculpting quality.
- Maintain neutral posture and core engagement during every set.
- Progress volume only when current week is completed with clean form.
- Manage breathing rhythm to sustain output over all rounds.

## Safety Protocol
- Stop immediately if pain appears.
- Prioritize control and form over speed.
- Adjust round count when recovery quality drops.
