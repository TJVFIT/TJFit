# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Gut Health Fat Loss Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Fat Loss + Digestion Improvement + Reduced Bloating  
Nutrition Style: Gut-Friendly Fat-Loss  
Daily Calories: ~1,900 kcal  
Hydration: 3-3.5L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week gut-health focused fat-loss nutrition system.
- Designed to improve digestion quality while producing steady fat reduction.
- Uses probiotic-friendly foods, clean protein sources, and controlled carbs.
- Built for sustainable adherence with simple, repeatable meals.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Yogurt Gut Bowl (probiotic yogurt, oats, honey, almonds, cinnamon).
- **Lunch:** Grilled Chicken and Rice Bowl.
- **Dinner:** Salmon with broccoli and carrots.
- **Snack:** Apple with peanut butter.

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Eggs and whole-grain toast.
- **Lunch:** Tuna Gut Salad with yogurt and lemon.
- **Dinner:** Chicken and sweet potato.

### Days 3-7
- Rotate the same core meal structures with chicken, beef, tuna, salmon, rice, potatoes, and vegetables.
- Keep digestion-friendly ingredients and stable meal timing.

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~130g protein / ~180g carbs / ~60g fat
- **Water:** 3-3.5L daily
- **Primary objective:** reduce bloating and normalize digestion while cutting fat

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Digestive Reset)
- Stabilize digestion and hydration.
- Reduce food choices that trigger bloating.
- Build compliance with clean, repeatable meals.

### Weeks 3-6 (Consistency and Fat-Loss Build)
- Keep meal execution tight and predictable.
- Maintain balanced macros and protein consistency.
- Support digestion with low-irritation food prep methods.

### Weeks 7-10 (Lean and Light Phase)
- Continue fat-loss momentum with strict adherence.
- Keep probiotic and whole-food structure consistent.
- Minimize random dietary changes.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Compliance)
- Finalize with strong consistency and digestion stability.
- Focus on leaner physique and long-term food habits.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Base foods: yogurt/kefir, oats, eggs, chicken, beef, fish, tuna, rice, potatoes, vegetables, almonds, olive oil.
- Batch-cook proteins and carbs for easier daily compliance.
- Use simple seasoning system to keep meals enjoyable.

## Supplements (Optional)
- Probiotics
- Whey protein
- Omega-3
- Multivitamin

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** less bloating, better digestive comfort
- **Weeks 3-6:** steady visible fat-loss trend
- **Weeks 8-12:** leaner and healthier look with better routine control

## Safety Protocol
- If digestive discomfort worsens, simplify meals and reassess triggers.
- Do not over-restrict calories.
- Keep hydration high and meal timing consistent.
