# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Clean Cutting Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Lose Fat, Keep Muscle, Get Lean and Defined  
Nutrition Style: Clean High-Protein Cutting  
Daily Calories: ~1,800 kcal  
Hydration: 3-3.5L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week clean cutting nutrition system focused on fat loss with muscle retention.
- Built around high-protein meals, controlled carbs, and clean food quality.
- Week 1 uses simple, premium meal templates for immediate consistency.
- Designed for an aesthetic and defined outcome without extreme dieting.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Protein Oat Bowl (oats, whey, low-fat milk, cinnamon, half banana).
- **Lunch:** Clean Chicken and Rice Plate with broccoli.
- **Dinner:** Lean Beef with zucchini and carrots.
- **Snack:** Greek yogurt with almonds.

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Eggs and whole-grain toast.
- **Lunch:** Tuna rice bowl.
- **Dinner:** Chicken and sweet potato.

### Days 3-7
- Repeat and rotate clean proteins (chicken, beef, tuna, fish), rice/sweet potato, and vegetables.
- Keep portions measured and meal timing stable.

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~150g protein / ~150g carbs / ~50g fat
- **Water:** 3-3.5L daily
- **Primary objective:** start fat loss while preserving muscle and training output

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Foundation)
- Lock in clean meal consistency and hydration.
- Build routine with repeatable high-protein templates.
- Expect early visual tightening and bodyweight drop.

### Weeks 3-6 (Momentum)
- Keep protein high and carbs controlled for performance.
- Continue meal-prep consistency and avoid random intake.
- Support steady, sustainable fat-loss trend.

### Weeks 7-10 (Definition)
- Tighten compliance and reduce meal variability.
- Preserve muscle quality while reducing body fat further.
- Maintain clean-food discipline under busy schedules.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Cut)
- Final high-consistency phase with stable macro control.
- Focus on a sharp, lean visual finish with routine discipline.
- Avoid drastic changes and rely on proven meal structure.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Core foods: oats, whey, milk, eggs, chicken, lean beef, tuna, fish, rice, sweet potatoes, Greek yogurt, vegetables, olive oil, almonds, banana.
- Batch-cook proteins and carbs to maintain compliance.
- Keep seasoning simple: salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, cinnamon.

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein
- Creatine
- Omega-3
- Multivitamin

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** weight drop and tighter look
- **Weeks 3-6:** visible fat-loss progression
- **Weeks 8-12:** sharper, aesthetic, defined physique

## Safety Protocol
- Do not over-restrict calories.
- Keep hydration and recovery consistent.
- Reduce intensity or adjust portions if fatigue accumulates.
