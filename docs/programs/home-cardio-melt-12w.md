# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## 12-Week Home Cardio Melt

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Home Training  
Primary Goal: Maximum Calorie Burn + Endurance  
Training Style: Cardio Dominant (Low Strength)  
Frequency: 5-6 Days / Week  
Equipment: None  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week cardio-dominant system designed for high calorie expenditure.
- Progression uses round scaling, rest reduction, HIIT blocks, and Tabata sets.
- Strength load is intentionally minimal to keep focus on cardio output.
- Includes low-intensity support days for recovery and consistency.

## Weekly Training Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Cardio Foundation)
1. Day 1 and Day 4: Steady cardio rounds (jog in place, jumping jacks, high knees).
2. Day 2 and Day 5: Light interval day (fast march + sprint intervals).
3. Day 3: Walk 25-30 min.
4. Day 6: Walk 30 min.
5. Day 7: Rest.

### Weeks 3-4 (Volume and Interval Upgrade)
1. Week 3 increases steady cardio rounds and interval rounds.
2. Week 4 raises jog and high-knee durations.
3. Rest intervals are reduced to increase density.
4. Day 6 transitions to light jog + walk blend.

### Weeks 5-6 (Density and Output)
1. Day 1 remains steady but adds rounds and tighter rest.
2. Day 2 sprint/walk intervals scale to higher total rounds.
3. Walking and jogging support continues for conditioning base.

### Weeks 7-8 (Higher Intensity Block)
1. Intervals increase in duration and pace demand.
2. Week 8 adds daily finisher:
   - Sprint 20 sec
   - Rest 10 sec
   - Repeat 8 rounds

### Weeks 9-10 (HIIT + Tabata Phase)
1. Day 1 uses 30 sec work / 15 sec rest model.
2. Day 2 uses Tabata sprint sets with controlled breaks.
3. Week 10 progresses round and set volume.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Phase)
1. Week 11 adds finisher:
   - Jumping Jacks 1 min
   - High Knees 1 min
   - Repeat 2 rounds
2. Week 12 Day 1 progresses to 40 sec work / 10 sec rest x 10 rounds.
3. Week 12 Day 2 Tabata progresses to 4 sets.
4. Support cardio peaks with walk 45 min and jog 25 min.

## Coaching Notes (Premium Delivery Standard)
- Maintain controlled breathing before increasing speed.
- Keep shoulders relaxed and core engaged through intervals.
- Track output consistency across all rounds rather than early over-pacing.
- Scale set count when recovery quality drops.

## Safety Protocol
- Stop immediately if dizziness or pain appears.
- Never continue with poor form under fatigue.
- Hydrate and recover fully between high-intensity sessions.
