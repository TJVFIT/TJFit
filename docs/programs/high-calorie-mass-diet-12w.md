# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## High Calorie Mass Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Fast Weight Gain + Maximum Muscle Growth  
Nutrition Style: High-Calorie Mass Gain  
Daily Calories: ~3,200-3,500 kcal  
Hydration: 3.5-4L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week high-calorie mass-gain nutrition system for rapid size and strength improvements.
- Built with high carbs for training energy, high protein for muscle building, and sufficient fats for recovery.
- Week 1 uses dense, practical meals to establish immediate surplus consistency.
- Designed for athletes who need faster scale and strength progression.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Mass Oat Power Bowl  
  80g oats, 1 scoop whey, 300ml milk, 1 banana, 1 tbsp peanut butter, 1 tbsp honey  
  Approx macros: 40P / 110C / 25F
- **Lunch:** Chicken Double Rice Bowl  
  200g chicken, 150g cooked rice, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, paprika  
  Approx macros: 55P / 100C / 25F
- **Dinner:** Beef and Potato Bulk Plate  
  200g beef, 300g potatoes, 1 tsp butter, salt, garlic powder  
  Approx macros: 50P / 90C / 25F
- **Snack:** Mass Shake  
  300ml milk, 1 scoop whey, 1 tbsp peanut butter  
  Approx macros: 35P / 30C / 20F

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Eggs and Oats Combo  
  4 eggs, 60g oats, 250ml milk  
  Approx macros: 40P / 70C / 30F
- **Lunch:** Tuna Rice Bulk  
  2 cans tuna, 150g rice, 1 tbsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 60P / 100C / 20F
- **Dinner:** Chicken Pasta  
  200g chicken, 120g pasta, 1 tbsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 55P / 100C / 25F

### Days 3-7
- **Day 3:** Mass oat bowl / Chicken + rice / Beef + potatoes
- **Day 4:** Eggs + oats / Tuna + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 5:** Mass oat bowl / Chicken + rice / Beef + potatoes
- **Day 6:** Eggs + oats / Tuna + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 7:** Mass oat bowl / Chicken + rice / Beef + potatoes

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~180g protein / ~400g carbs / ~100g fat
- **Water:** 3.5-4L daily
- **Primary objective:** establish a stable high-calorie surplus for fast growth

## Week 1 Quick Recipe Standards
- **Mass Oat Bowl:** cook oats and milk 5-6 min, remove from heat, then add whey, banana, peanut butter, and honey.
- **Chicken Double Rice Bowl:** season chicken, cook on medium-high 6-7 min each side, boil rice 10-12 min, finish with olive oil.
- **Beef and Potato Plate:** bake potatoes at 200C for 30-35 min; sear beef on high heat 3-4 min each side; finish with butter and garlic.
- **Chicken Pasta:** boil pasta 8-10 min, cook chicken, mix with olive oil.

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Mass Base)
- Build consistent high-calorie execution.
- Adapt appetite and digestion to larger meal volume.
- Expect fast bodyweight increase.

### Weeks 3-6 (Growth Block)
- Keep calorie surplus high and measurable.
- Maintain high protein and high training-energy carbs.
- Track strength progression and bodyweight trend.

### Weeks 7-10 (Heavy Gain Phase)
- Sustain intake with low meal variability.
- Use shake support when appetite is low.
- Keep growth focused and performance-driven.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Mass Finish)
- Finalize with strict surplus consistency.
- Preserve training performance and recovery quality.
- Target clear visual size increase and stronger lifts.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Core items: oats, whey, milk (large amount), eggs, chicken (~2kg), beef (~1.2kg), tuna cans (5-6), rice (1.5kg), pasta, potatoes (2-3kg), peanut butter, honey, olive oil, butter.
- Batch-cook high-volume proteins and carb sources to avoid missed intake.
- Keep at least one liquid-calorie option ready daily.

## Alternatives
- Chicken -> turkey or beef
- Beef -> chicken thighs
- Rice -> pasta or bread
- Milk -> water (less calories)
- Peanut butter -> nuts
- Whey -> eggs

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein
- Creatine
- Mass gainer
- Omega-3

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** fast weight gain
- **Weeks 3-6:** noticeable strength increase
- **Weeks 8-12:** visible size and mass improvement

## Safety Protocol
- Prioritize quality calorie sources, not only junk food.
- If digestion is stressed, spread meals into smaller feedings.
- Adjust surplus if fat gain outpaces performance gains.
