# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Lean Bulk Diet (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Nutrition Program  
Primary Goal: Build Muscle While Staying Lean  
Nutrition Style: Controlled Lean Bulk  
Daily Calories: ~2,600-2,800 kcal  
Hydration: 3-4L Water Daily  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week lean-bulk nutrition system for quality muscle gain.
- Combines high protein, performance carbs, and controlled fats.
- Designed to support gym performance while limiting unnecessary fat gain.
- Uses practical, repeatable meals for long-term adherence.

## Week 1 Premium Meal Framework

### Day 1
- **Breakfast:** Muscle Oat Bowl  
  60g oats, 1 scoop whey, 250ml milk, 1 banana, 1 tbsp peanut butter  
  Approx macros: 35P / 75C / 18F
- **Lunch:** Chicken Rice Bulk Bowl  
  180g chicken, 120g cooked rice, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika  
  Approx macros: 50P / 80C / 20F
- **Dinner:** Beef and Potato Power Meal  
  180g beef, 250g potatoes, 1 tsp butter, salt, garlic powder  
  Approx macros: 45P / 70C / 20F
- **Snack:** Yogurt and Nuts  
  200g yogurt, 20g almonds  
  Approx macros: 20P / 20C / 18F

### Day 2
- **Breakfast:** Eggs and Oats Combo  
  3 eggs, 40g oats, 200ml milk  
  Approx macros: 30P / 60C / 20F
- **Lunch:** Tuna Rice Bowl  
  1 can tuna, 120g rice, 1 tbsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 40P / 80C / 15F
- **Dinner:** Chicken and Pasta  
  180g chicken, 100g pasta, 1 tsp olive oil  
  Approx macros: 50P / 80C / 15F

### Days 3-7
- **Day 3:** Oat bowl / Chicken + rice / Beef + potato
- **Day 4:** Eggs + oats / Tuna + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 5:** Oat bowl / Chicken + rice / Beef + potato
- **Day 6:** Eggs + oats / Tuna + rice / Chicken + pasta
- **Day 7:** Oat bowl / Chicken + rice / Beef + potato

## Week 1 Targets
- **Macros (daily avg):** ~160g protein / ~320g carbs / ~70g fat
- **Water:** 3-4L daily
- **Primary objective:** establish stable surplus without digestive stress

## Week 1 Quick Recipe Standards
- **Muscle Oat Bowl:** cook oats with milk 5 min on medium heat, remove from heat, then add whey, banana, and peanut butter.
- **Chicken Rice Bulk Bowl:** season chicken with salt, paprika, and pepper; cook 5-6 min each side; boil rice 10-12 min; top with olive oil.
- **Beef and Potato Power Meal:** bake potatoes at 200C for about 30 min; cook beef on high heat 3-4 min each side; finish with butter and garlic.
- **Chicken and Pasta:** boil pasta 8-10 min; cook chicken as above; combine and season.

## 12-Week Progression Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Lean Bulk Base)
- Build consistency with full daily calorie target.
- Stabilize appetite and digestion with routine meal timing.
- Start tracking bodyweight trend and training output.

### Weeks 3-6 (Growth Momentum)
- Maintain surplus and protein precision.
- Keep carbs centered around workouts for performance.
- Improve strength and workload in gym sessions.

### Weeks 7-10 (Lean Gain Refinement)
- Continue growth while controlling fat gain pace.
- Keep food quality high and avoid random junk surplus.
- Make small adjustments only when trend data supports it.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Lean Gain Discipline)
- Finish with strict consistency and performance support.
- Lock in high-protein habits and recovery structure.
- Target visible muscle gain with controlled body-fat increase.

## Shopping and Prep Notes
- Core items: oats, whey, milk, eggs, chicken (~1.5-2kg), beef (~1kg), tuna cans, rice, pasta, potatoes, yogurt, almonds, peanut butter, olive oil, butter.
- Batch-cook proteins and carbs to avoid missed intake.
- Keep easy snack options ready for high-adherence days.

## Alternatives
- Chicken -> turkey or tuna
- Beef -> chicken thighs
- Rice -> pasta or potatoes
- Milk -> water or almond milk
- Peanut butter -> almonds
- Yogurt -> milk or skip

## Supplements (Optional)
- Whey protein
- Creatine
- Omega-3
- Multivitamin

## Expected Outcome Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** slight weight increase from surplus adaptation
- **Weeks 3-6:** improved strength and training performance
- **Weeks 8-12:** visible quality muscle gain with leaner bulk profile

## Safety Protocol
- Do not force excessive calorie jumps.
- Spread meals across the day for better digestion and compliance.
- Adjust surplus gradually if fat gain increases too fast.
