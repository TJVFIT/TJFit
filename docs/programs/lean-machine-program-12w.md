# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Lean Machine Program (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Gym Training  
Primary Goal: Fat Loss + Lean Aesthetic Physique  
Training Style: Moderate Weights + Volume + Shaping  
Frequency: 5-6 Days / Week  
Equipment: Full Gym  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week gym shaping protocol designed to build a lean and aesthetic look.
- Uses structured split training with progressive volume and controlled cardio.
- Includes supersets, timed density blocks, and finishers for physique detail.
- Built to drive fat loss while preserving and refining muscle shape.

## Weekly Training Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Foundation Block)
1. Day 1 and Day 5: Chest-focused shaping plus cardio.
2. Day 2: Back-focused shaping plus cardio.
3. Day 3: Leg-focused shaping plus cardio.
4. Day 4: Core plus cardio.
5. Day 6: Light cardio support.
6. Day 7: Rest.

### Weeks 3-4 (Volume and Density Build)
1. Week 3 scales all main sessions to 4 sets.
2. Week 4 keeps 4 sets and reduces rest to improve density.
3. Cardio baseline stabilizes around 20 minutes.

### Weeks 5-6 (Shaping Superset Phase)
1. Chest, back, and leg days move into targeted superset structures.
2. Core day adds denser pairing structure.
3. Week 6 progresses to 5 sets with longer cardio support.

### Weeks 7-8 (Lean Build)
1. Rep targets increase while rest windows narrow.
2. Week 8 adds finisher:
   - Jump Squats x 10
   - Push-ups x 12
   - Repeat 3 rounds

### Weeks 9-10 (Detail Phase)
1. Timed structure begins:
   - 40 sec work / 20 sec rest
2. Applied across chest, back, legs, and core sessions.
3. Week 10 increases to 7 rounds.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Aesthetic)
1. Week 11 finisher:
   - Push-ups x 15
   - Jump Squats x 12
   - Repeat 3 rounds
2. Week 12 shifts key blocks to:
   - 45 sec work / 15 sec rest
   - 7 rounds
3. Day 6 cardio extends to 40 minutes.

## Coaching Notes (Premium Delivery Standard)
- Maintain strict form to keep muscle shape and symmetry under fatigue.
- Use cardio to support composition goals, not to compromise lifting quality.
- Track weekly density progression (sets, rounds, rest discipline).
- Keep technique clean on every rep, especially in timed phases.

## Safety Protocol
- Focus on control and form in every block.
- Do not rush repetitions.
- Stop immediately when technique deteriorates.
