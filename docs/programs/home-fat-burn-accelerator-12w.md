# TJFIT PROGRAM DOSSIER
## Home Fat Burn Accelerator (12 Weeks)

--------------------------------------------
Program Type: Home Training  
Primary Goal: Fat Loss + Cardio  
Level: Beginner -> Advanced  
Equipment: None  
--------------------------------------------

## Executive Overview
- 12-week progressive bodyweight system built for sustainable fat loss.
- Movement quality first, then density, then peak conditioning.
- Clear day-by-day structure with planned walking/jogging support.
- Recovery and safety are embedded to prevent burnout.

## Weekly Training Framework

### Weeks 1-2 (Foundation Block)
1. Day 1: Jumping Jacks, Squats, High Knees, Push-ups, Plank -> 3 rounds
2. Day 2: Mountain Climbers, Lunges, Burpees, Sit-ups -> 3 rounds
3. Day 3: Walk 20-25 min
4. Day 4: Repeat Day 1
5. Day 5: Repeat Day 2
6. Day 6: Walk 25-30 min
7. Day 7: Rest

### Weeks 3-4 (Volume Block)
1. Day 1 and Day 2 move from 3 rounds to 4 rounds.
2. Work intervals rise to 40 sec in key conditioning movements.
3. Walking volume rises to 30-35 min.
4. Week 4 Day 6 includes light jog + walk blend.

### Weeks 5-6 (Density Block)
1. Main sessions continue at 4 rounds, then scale to 5 rounds.
2. Rest periods reduced to 15-20 sec in work circuits.
3. Cardio progression: walking to structured jogging (15-20 min).
4. Core and push volume increase with quality control.

### Weeks 7-8 (HIIT Block)
1. Main work intervals increase to 45 sec in high-output drills.
2. Week 8 finisher after workouts:
   - Burpees x 10
   - Jump Squats x 10
   - Repeat 3 rounds
3. Base walking/jogging support remains in place for recovery.

### Weeks 9-10 (Timed Structure Block)
1. Shift to 40 sec work / 20 sec rest format.
2. Main sessions move from 6 rounds to 7 rounds.
3. Conditioning support days remain active.

### Weeks 11-12 (Peak Block)
1. Week 11 adds second finisher:
   - High Knees 30 sec
   - Plank 30 sec
   - Repeat 3 rounds
2. Week 12 peak sessions:
   - 45 sec work / 15 sec rest
   - 7 rounds
3. Day 3 walk: 45 min
4. Day 6 jog: 25 min

## Coaching Notes (Premium Delivery Standard)
- Keep movement standards strict before pace progression.
- Use breathing rhythm to control heart rate between rounds.
- Stop all sessions if pain, dizziness, or loss of stability appears.
- Hydrate before and after all sessions.

## Safety Protocol
- Immediate stop triggers: sharp pain, dizziness, vision changes, joint instability.
- Scale volume down if fatigue accumulates for more than 2 consecutive days.
- Prioritize sleep and hydration to maintain performance.
